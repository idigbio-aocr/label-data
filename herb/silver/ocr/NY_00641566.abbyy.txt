PUERTO RICO
Gramineae Bambusa tuldoides Munro
LOCALITY: Mayagüez: Federal Experiment Station.
HABITAT: Cultivated.
DESCRIPTION: Clump dense, narrow erect culms to 20m tall, 2-3 cm diameter. New culm leaves deciduous, glabrous, stramineous, the blade erect with two lateral ciliate appendages. Spikelets produced at first on leafy branches. See field book for further description
